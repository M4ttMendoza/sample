Five Noli Me Tangere Characters

Padre Damaso Padre Damaso was a brazen and haughty man. He acted superior to others due to the authority that came with becoming a friar and opposed locals going overseas to study.
Kapitan Tiago He is a wealthy native Filipino who interacted with influential people at that time. He stands in for the wealthy Filipinos who take advantage of their own people in order to gain influence and wealth via their close connections.
Maria Clara When María Clara was younger, she was referred to as everyone's idol since she grew up in a loving and happy environment.
Crisostomo Ibarra Crisostomo Ibarra was a young man with high aspirations. He wanted to develop the nation, influenced by his European education.
Elías Elias was a selfless person who upheld the honor code. Growing up wealthy, he was initially an arrogant individual
